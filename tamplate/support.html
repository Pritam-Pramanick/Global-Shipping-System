Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to our most common inquiries.

To file a cargo claim, navigate to your Dashboard, select the specific shipment, and click "File Exception/Claim". Please ensure you have photos of the damaged goods and the original commercial invoice ready to upload. Claims must be filed within 14 days of delivery.
A customs hold usually means the destination country requires additional documentation or duty payments before releasing the cargo. Check the "Documents" tab on your shipment profile. If an action is required from you, it will be highlighted in red.
Yes, "In-Transit Rerouting" is available for Air and Ground transport. However, this may incur additional fees and affect the ETA. Please submit a support ticket using the form on this page with the subject "Reroute Request" for immediate assistance.
API access requires an Enterprise tier account. If you are an Enterprise user, go to "My Profile" > "Developer Settings" to generate your secure API keys and access our Postman documentation.
