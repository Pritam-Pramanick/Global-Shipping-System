Our Mission

At Trans Global, we believe that logistics should be completely invisible to your end customer. You shouldn't have to worry about where your cargo is—it should just be where you need it, exactly when you need it.

Founded in 2011, we have built a network that spans over 120 countries. We utilize AI-driven route optimization and real-time IoT tracking to eliminate the traditional "black holes" of international shipping. We don't just move boxes; we engineer global trade.

Goal: Zero-Carbon
by 2040.

The global shipping industry accounts for nearly 3% of global CO2 emissions. We are committed to changing that narrative.

Our Eco-Route Initiative utilizes wind-assisted vessel propulsion, Bio-LNG fuels, and AI-optimized speed reduction. For emissions we cannot eliminate today, we automatically purchase verified carbon offsets.
