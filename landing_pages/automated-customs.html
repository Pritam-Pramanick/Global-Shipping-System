Frictionless Cross-Border Trade

The biggest delays in global supply chains don't happen on the ocean—they happen at the border due to paperwork errors. Trans Global replaces manual entry with machine-learning compliance engines that pre-clear your cargo while it is still in transit.

  • Smart OCR Document Parsing

    Upload your commercial invoices. Our AI instantly extracts HS codes, values, and origins, validating them against real-time global tariff databases to prevent misclassifications.

  • Predictive Duty Optimization

    Our algorithms automatically calculate exact duties, taxes, and port fees, ensuring you never overpay or get hit with unexpected terminal charges upon arrival.

  • Blockchain Bill of Lading

    We issue tamper-proof, immutable digital ledgers for all shipping documents, allowing customs authorities to instantly verify cargo origin and ownership.
