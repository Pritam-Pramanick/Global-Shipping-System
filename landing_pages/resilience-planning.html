The 4 Pillars of Contingency

We deploy cross-functional teams to build "Plan B" networks that can be activated at a moment's notice, ensuring zero downtime for your production lines.

Multi-Modal Fallbacks

When ocean lanes choke, we instantly pivot your high-priority cargo to pre-secured air charter networks, ensuring critical components still arrive on time.

Secondary Carrier Contracts

We secure legally binding "standby" capacity with secondary global carriers, ensuring you always have trucks and ships available even during capacity crunches.

Geopolitical Risk Monitoring

Our AI monitors global news, tariff shifts, and labor union strikes in real-time, automatically triggering reroutes before the bottleneck actually occurs.

Strategic Buffer Staging

We mathematically calculate and deploy safety stock to secure bonded warehouses near your final assembly points to absorb the shock of sudden supplier outages.
